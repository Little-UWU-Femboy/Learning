We want to calculate \(139 - 79\) step by step with borrowing:

$$
\begin{array}{r@{}r@{}r}
  & 1 & 3 & 9 \\  % hundreds, tens, ones
- &   & 7 & 9 \\ \hline
\end{array}
$$

Step 1: Subtract the ones place \(9 - 9 = 0\)

$$
\begin{array}{r@{}r@{}r}
  & 1 & 3 & 9 \\
- &   & 7 & 9 \\ \hline
  &   &   & 0
\end{array}
$$

Step 2: Subtract the tens place. Since \(3 - 7\) cannot be done, we borrow 1 from the hundreds place. The 1 in the hundreds becomes 0, and the tens 3 becomes 13:

$$
\begin{array}{r@{}r@{}r}
  & 0 & 13 & 9 \\
- &   &  7 & 9 \\ \hline
  &   &  6 & 0
\end{array}
$$

Step 3: Subtract the hundreds place: \(0 - 0 = 0\)

Finally, we get:

$$
139 - 79 = 60
$$
