# Before The Coffee Gets Cold

## Characters

### Fumiko

One of the main characters of the story. Her was boyfriend (*Goro*) left to go work in America.

### Goro

Used to be the boyfriend of *Fumiko*. Now works in America.

### Kazu Tokita

Works at the coffee shop as a waitress. Knows how the. Time travel works

### Yaeko Hirai

Women who always goes to the shop. She knows about the time travel and its rules.



## Extra Information

- Coffee Shop name is <u>Funiculi Funicula</u>.
- Rules for time travel are:
    1. Can go back in time, but no matter what is done the present will never change
    2. Can only visit people who visited the coffee shop before
    3. Only one seat can send the person to the past. That seat cannot be left at all while in the past
    4. Time limit in visiting the past
